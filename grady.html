Grady's Gamer Profile

About Me

I started playing video games when I was about four years old. The first console I played on was the PlayStation Two, released on 2000 October 26. I have played on my consoles, including Playstation One, Two, Three, Four, and the Wii. Since 2020, I have moved away from console gaming and now play exclusively on PC. I mostly play single and multiplayer first-person shooter games.

Favourite Game

DayZ

DayZ is an open-world survival game that focuses on realism and challenging gameplay. It was originally a mod for a military game called ARMA 2 but later became a standalone game. In DayZ, players are dropped into a fictional city called Chernarus, which zombies have overrun after an apocalypse. The goal in DayZ is simple: survive as long as possible against zombies, environmental dangers, and other players.
