Sean's Gamer Profile

Introduction

I've been gaming since 2012, but if you ask me when I really started gaming, I'd say it was on my Grandma's Nokia phone. I played Snake on her low-pixel screen, a nostalgic pastime that I still love. These days, I play League of Legends, Minecraft, and Roblox on my PC.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game with a blocky aesthetic. By yourself, it is a relaxing way to game and unwind. With friends, it becomes a fun adventure.

Fun Fact
